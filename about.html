About B3R Chamber

Who We Are

B3R Chamber is an independent interdisciplinary research and policy platform connecting science, technology, artificial intelligence, public health, and evidence-based policymaking.

Our Mission

To bridge research, innovation, and public policy by producing evidence-based analysis that supports informed decision-making.

Our Vision

To become a trusted interdisciplinary platform advancing science-informed policy, responsible innovation, and sustainable development.

Our Values

Integrity

Research grounded in evidence and transparency.

Collaboration

Connecting academia, policy, industry, and society.

Impact

Transforming knowledge into practical solutions.

Leadership

Ms. Bandana Neupane

Founder & Executive Director

Ms. Bandana Neupane is a public health professional, registered nurse, and data science enthusiast with a strong interest in evidence-based healthcare, digital health, artificial intelligence, and interdisciplinary research. She is passionate about leveraging data, technology, and innovation to improve public health outcomes, strengthen healthcare systems, and support informed policymaking.

As Founder & Executive Director of B3R Chamber (Bodha: The Third Room), she leads the organization's mission to advance independent interdisciplinary research, foster collaboration, and translate evidence into practical solutions that benefit society.
